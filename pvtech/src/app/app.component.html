Meet the Team

We Are PV Technology

The fast growing Web development and QA Consulting Services company providing Offshore Services. We are highly structured, performance oriented and customer centric organization. We build web solutions, which evolve with the changing needs of your business. PV Technology is comprised of a diverse and eclectic group of individuals who see the world a little differently in a good way!

Vision

To develop in a constant manner and grow as a major IT service provider to become a leading performer, in providing quality Web and Software Development solutions in the competitive global marketplace.

Mission

To enhancing the business growth of our customers with creative Design and Development to deliver market-defining high-quality solutions that create value and reliable competitive advantage for our clients around the world.

What We Do

Software and Web Development, Testing & QA, E-Commerce, Mobile Application, and SEO & SEM. We are developing the software technologies like PHP, ASP.NET, Java, Mobile Applications, C# and Graphics Designs.

Software Development

The wide span of our expertise across PV Technology and platforms enables us to handle any kind of IT requirement today. We keep up with the latest IT trends. We have a team of hardworking, motivated, and a good infrastructure to help us achieve technically advanced and customer-oriented solutions.
